MY EXPERIENCE

Everyone must have a sweet or bitter experience in their life including me.There are some sweet experiences that I have gone through throughout my life that is when I got an excellent results in primary school assessment tests that made my parents feel very proud and impressed with me. Next, when my birthday arrive, my bestfriends will always make a birthday celebration and give some presents for me that will make me feel very happy because even though they have their own lives but they never forget me. Besides, a sweet experience that I will never forget when I got the dean’s results four times in a rows in the final exams starting from semester 1 and it made me feel very glad with myself because despite having to compete with outstanding students at the university, I was still able to achieve the dean’s award. Last but not least, the sweetest experience I have ever had was when I was with my family in the village, at that time my family and I gathered to celebrate the Eid together.

Apart from that, I also had a bitter experience in my life which is when I was 15 years old, I had a rather serious road accident that resulted in my leg being broken and made me unable to walk for two months. In addition, when i lost my grandfather and i did not get to see her body buried because i was not allowed to cross the state because of covid 19 disease but I am so thankful that I was able to meet him before he passed away even though his condition was very critical at that time.
